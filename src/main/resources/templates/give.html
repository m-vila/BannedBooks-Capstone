WHY DONATE TO PEN AMERICA?

PEN America is the leading organization fighting book bans, protecting the freedom to read, and ensuring that people everywhere have the freedom to create literature, convey information and ideas, and express their views. A donation to PEN America supports our work to protect writers and access to books, celebrate literature, and defend free speech. Please join us today.

Help us continue to champion the freedom to write and celebrate essential voices of literature by making an online donation to PEN America today, becoming a PEN America Member, making a gift in honor of a loved one, leaving a legacy gift, or supporting one of our signature events.

"If you only read the books that everyone else is reading,
you can only think what everyone else is thinking."

Haruki Murakami

BECOME A PEN AMERICA YOUNG PATRON

Young Patrons are supporters in their 20s and 30s who care deeply about PEN America’s mission and are interested in participating in networking events, fundraising, and PEN’s flagship literary events with like-minded peers.

As budding philanthropists, the PEN Young Patrons host a series of events and writer-centric programs that help identify, support, and elevate emerging creative voices whose work stands at the intersection of literary freedom and political engagement.

Young Patrons will gain early access and invitations to PEN America’s most exciting events, including the Literary Gala Afterparty, a special Young Patrons networking event at the end of the year, and other opportunities to network with passionate readers, writers, creatives, and defenders of free expression.
